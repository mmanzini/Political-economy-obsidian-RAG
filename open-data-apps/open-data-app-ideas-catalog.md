## Key Takeaways

- The top of the list is dominated by **NL-specific** apps where the open-data ecosystem is unusually mature.
- High feasibility almost always wins over high theoretical revenue — an EU-wide ambition with a fragile API scores worse than a clean NL niche.
- Launch in batches of 3–4 so the shared Flutter template gets stress-tested early.
