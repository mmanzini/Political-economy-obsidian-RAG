# European Christian Nationalism: Meloni, Salvini, and the Italian Template

*Sources: Canopy Forum (2024); Springer (2025, Fratelli d'Italia); USCIRF source material; compiled 2026.*

## The Italian Template

- **Giorgia Meloni** (Prime Minister, Fratelli d'Italia / Brothers of Italy):
  - Launched international profile with 2019 viral speech: "I am Giorgia, I am a woman, I am a mother, I am Italian, I am Christian, and no one will take that away from me"
  - Religious identity politics: Christianity as civilisational bulwark against Islam; Catholic values against "gender ideology"
  - Academic framing: employs women's rights rhetoric instrumentally — as a vehicle for anti-Islam exclusion rather than feminist emancipation (Springer, 2025)
  - Consecrated Italy to the Immaculate Heart of Mary — ritual political-religious theatre
- **Matteo Salvini** (Lega Nord, Deputy PM):
  - More explicitly far-right; pan-European Christian Nationalism
  - Carries rosary beads and crucifix at rallies; slogan: "first Italy, then God"
  - Stronger ties to Orbán's international network; maintains pro-Russia positions on Ukraine

## Structural Parallel with MAGA

| Dimension | MAGA / Kirk (US) | Meloni/Salvini (Italy) |
|-----------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Religious framing | Christianity under siege by secular progressivism | Christianity as identity against Islam and globalism |
| Enemy | "Marxist elites," LGBTQ agenda | Islam, EU technocracy, gender ideology |
| Rhetorical mode | Grace-vs-sword (Kirk); apocalyptic | Civilisational (Meloni); populist-nativist (Salvini) |
| Institutional relationship | MAGA hostile to established churches | FdI/Lega exploit Catholic symbolism; not always aligned with Vatican |
| International network | National Conservatism conferences; Orbán axis | ECR (European Conservatives and Reformists); ties to Orbán |

## Key Divergence: Meloni's "Moderate Abroad"

- In government, Meloni has adopted a relatively conventional foreign policy stance: pro-NATO, pro-Ukraine; moderating the radical base for credibility
- Academic framing (Springer, 2025): "Moderate abroad, radical at home" — FdI governs differently than it campaigns
- Salvini has maintained more independent, pro-Russia positions on Ukraine, creating intra-coalition tension
- The divergence reveals the same strategic pragmatism as Trump's predatory doctrine: radical posture as political mobilisation, institutional behaviour as separate register

## The Castellio-Calvin Echo

- The [[maga-new-religious-movement]] article uses the Castellio vs Calvin frame: grace vs coercive orthodoxy (tolerance vs enforced belonging)
- Italian parallel: Meloni's invocation of Christian civilisation is Calvinist in structure (identity enforcement) even if Catholic in form
- Salvini is more explicitly coercive: police powers, immigration detention framed as civilisational and religious protection
- Both represent a shift from the Castellio pole (mercy, pluralism) toward the Calvin pole (enforced belonging)
- Italy is the historical laboratory: Mussolini's Lateran Pacts (1929) established the template of instrumentalising the Church for political legitimacy — Meloni and Salvini operate in a long tradition

## Pan-European Network

- ECR (European Conservatives and Reformists): Meloni's FdI is the lead party; largest-right-of-EPP grouping in the European Parliament
- Orbán (Fidesz / Hungary): the longest-serving exemplar of Christian nationalist governance in Europe; provides the template Meloni and Salvini adapt
- Cross-Atlantic links: National Conservatism conferences convene US (MAGA), UK (Conservative right), Italian, and Hungarian figures on shared platforms
- The network is not merely ideological — it shares advisers, messaging strategies, and funding sources

## Key Takeaways

- Meloni and Salvini are not importing MAGA — they are applying a pre-existing Italian tradition of religiously inflected identity politics, updated for the 2020s global right-wing wave
- The structural move is identical to MAGA: religion as mobilisation tool, not theological conviction; form preserved, substance replaced by nationalism
- Meloni's "moderate abroad, radical at home" pattern is a more disciplined version of the same dual register that MAGA exploits — utility over consistency
- The Castellio-Calvin axis applies cross-nationally: the enforced-belonging pole is ascendant in both the US and Italy, as in Hungary
- Pan-European Christian Nationalism is institutionalised: ECR bloc, Orbán axis, National Conservatism conferences — not a collection of national oddities but a coordinated political project
