# Banking Predictions 2026: AI & the Future of Banking

Backbase's annual report on where banking is heading. The through-line: 2026 is when AI moves from pilots to productivity, with structural rather than incremental shifts across every banking segment.

## 1. From Pilots to Productivity
- Only **8% of banks** were doing strategic, enterprise-wide GenAI in late 2024 (IBM); 78% in tactical mode.
- **82% of US banks** plan to grow AI budget share (KPMG); ~40% expect AI to exceed 20% of total budget.
- McKinsey: banks must "move beyond experimentation"; multi-agent systems are key.
- **Citi now requires 175,000 employees to complete AI training.**
- Backbase view: first wins in customer servicing and compliance; SDLC sees the most dramatic change (one regional bank saw **40% developer productivity gain**).

## 2. Trust in the Age of Deepfakes
- Deloitte: GenAI-enabled fraud losses could hit **$40B by 2027** in the US (from $27B in 2023).
- Deepfake fraud attempts up **2,137%** in three years (Signicat).
- **>50% of detected fraud now involves AI** (Feedzai).
- Only 36% of banks "somewhat confident" in current fraud controls.
- 90% of FIs now using AI to fight fraud (Feedzai).
- Backbase view: trust becomes the defining competitive edge. Continuous verification (behavioral biometrics + risk-based step-ups), content-authenticity controls (liveness, deepfake analysis, watermarking), and **visible** trust (anti-scam education, transparent resolution timelines).

## 3. The Invisible Payments Economy
- US: RTP surpassed 1B transactions early 2025; FedNow expanding.
- EU: Instant Payments Regulation mandates instant euro transfers.
- **Brazil's Pix reaches ~75% of population.**
- **4.3B digital wallet users** in 2024 = 53% of global population (Capital One).
- Wallets account for ~30% of global in-store spend (McKinsey).
- Payments revenue: **$2.5T annually** from $2 quadrillion in flows across 3.6T transactions.
- Backbase view: payments become embedded, invisible, intelligent. Banks earn from **orchestration** (cards + accounts + wallets + digital currencies), instant settlement, programmable money, FX optimization.

## 4. Open Finance Acceleration
- 80% of banks plan open banking investment by 2025; usage to double by 2027 (Mastercard).
- **Embedded finance: $104.8B (2024) → $834B+ by 2034**, ~23% CAGR.
- BaaS to surpass $73B by 2034.
- Backbase view: open banking was the **launchpad, not the destination**. Winners build scalable API platforms that monetize ecosystem access; data/analytics become active revenue streams.

## 5. Race for Relevance vs Neobanks
- Neobanking: $210B (2025) → **$3.4T by 2032**, 48.9% CAGR.
- BCG: **only 1 in 4 banks** uses AI for competitive advantage.
- "Predictive, generative, and agentic AI are redefining the foundations of scale, efficiency, and customer experience" (BCG).
- Backbase view: incumbents need unified AI-powered platforms (not legacy silos), platform mindset (open APIs, deep partnerships, embedded services), and experience as the differentiator.

## Segment-Level Outlook

### Retail — Proactive, Personal, Invisible
- 85% of execs believe AI will significantly transform the industry within 5 years.
- BofA's Erica: **2.5B client interactions**, 20M customers.
- Capgemini: instant payments grow from 16% (2023) → 22% (2028) of volume.
- Banks become **active partners in customers' financial lives** (financial wellbeing coaching as differentiator).

### Private Banking — Augmented Relationships
- Some banks already run **100+ ML algorithms** on 15,000 customer data points for nudges (Acuity).
- EY: transformation hinges on combining human expertise with data-driven advisory.
- RM elevated to **strategic orchestrator**; AI copilots multiply reach; 64% of HNWIs concerned about lack of personalized advice (Capgemini).

### Wealth Management — Hyper-Automation Meets Personalization
- **~42% of wealth management work could be redefined by AI by 2030.**
- Hybrid advisory (human + digital) becomes baseline.
- Personalization at scale extends "high-touch" service beyond UHNWIs to mass affluent.
- Values-based / thematic / ESG investing becomes mainstream expectation.

### Small Business Banking — Relationship Banking, Redefined
- Embedded finance TAM: **$185B in US/EU**.
- AI/alternative-data scoring to serve thin-file SMBs.
- McKinsey: digital experience is now the **top reason** MSMEs choose primary bank.
- Banks shift from product delivery to **orchestration of an ecosystem**: payments, insurance, working-capital credit embedded into accounting/logistics/marketplace tools.
- Levers: modular architecture, dynamic credit, segment sensitivity, alternative-data scoring, AI governance.

### Commercial Banking — Predictive, Modular, Growth-Driven
- **70% of commercial banks** have adopted AI in at least one core function; 78% report positive ROI within 18 months.
- Datos Insights: shift from defensive to offensive — real-time payments, API-first infra, agentic AI as price of admission.
- API-led treasury and balance-sheet orchestration (joint optimization of liquidity, payments, FX, working capital).
- ESG as revenue (green asset factories, ESG-linked lending, carbon tracking) — no longer optional.
- Banks act less like monolithic institutions, more like **modular platforms**.

## Key Takeaways
- 2026's defining shift: AI moves from "productivity theater" to **measurable ROI** — but only for banks that embed agentic assistance, workflow automation, and AI-driven compliance in daily operations.
- **Trust** is the unified theme — across deepfake defense, AI explainability, payment reliability, and advisory transparency.
- Every segment is converging on the same playbook: **unified platform + AI orchestration + embedded delivery** where customers already are.
- The architectural foundation that makes all of this deployable is the [[ai-native-banking-os|AI-Native Banking OS]] — fragmentation is no longer survivable.
- The competitive gap is widening fast: 1-in-4 banks using AI strategically vs neobanks growing 48.9% CAGR. The window to act decisively is 2026.
